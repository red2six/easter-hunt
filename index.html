THE HUNT — STEP BY STEP

!IMPORTANT — before you start: Collect all 10 Wimpy Kid books and keep them close! You'll need to search through them to find hidden words. Check the book list on the right — do not begin until you have them all ready!
1You'll be shown a maths question — solve it to reveal a page from a Wimpy Kid book. Part of the text on that page is blacked out — that's your target word!
2Enter your answer to the maths question. Get it right and the exact page number in the real book is revealed — along with the blacked-out page!
3Find the actual book, turn to that page, and read the blacked-out word. Figure out which book it is yourself — that's the challenge!
4Type the hidden word into the HUNT page word box — if correct it goes straight into the crossword. Check progress under CROSSWORD tab!
5As you solve words, gold squares appear in the crossword. These are scrambled — write them down in order and use the cipher table in DECODER to decode each letter on paper.
6The 4 decoded words are number words. Enter their digits in the PADLOCK CODE box to check — then use the code on the real padlock! 🏆

TIPS FROM GREG

🥚 BONUS EGGS: Hidden around the house are special surprises! Bonus clues unlock as you solve more pages — check the EGGS tab!
⭐ GOLD SQUARES: Certain squares glow gold as you solve more words. Write down all gold letters in order — they spell scrambled number words. Decode each letter using the cipher table in the DECODER tab!
